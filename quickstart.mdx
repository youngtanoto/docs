---
title: "60 Day Breakdown"
description: "Young's first 60 days broken down into weeks. "
---

<Info>
  Accounts for a reduced content output during the handoff period, then gradually increased output toward weeks 5-8 and beyond.
</Info>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Week 1: Onboarding Process" stepNumber={1}>
    - Introductory calls with founders, marketing, product, customer success, technical writer, etc.
    - Tutorials/demos, familiarize further with platform
    - Setting up shared calendar with product team for new features
    - Platform access (CMS, analytics, social, etc.)
    - Review relevant materials, including:
      - Branding guidelines
      - Current marketing practices/past intiatives
      - Competitor analysis
      - Existing customer data
      - Slack community
      - Marketing KPI dashboard
      - Testimonials and case studies
      - Email sequences

    <Check>
      **Deliverables**: Mutual vibes, understanding of the platform/internal processes
    </Check>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Week 2: Research + Discovery">
    - Exploration calls with power users: 2-3 developers, 2-3 enterprise PMs/technical writers
    - Update user personas
    - Brand voice guidelines update
    - Set target channels and KPIs
    - Content audit \+ identify content gaps
    - Define new content pillars, content mix
    - Take over performance tracking for relevant channels
    - Integrate into interview process for Han's thought leadership ghostwriting
    - Set up basic content review process with Tiffany/team
    - Create 3 content pieces (2 social posts, 1 blog) using existing topic pipeline to establish workflow/content quality standards

    <Check>
      **Deliverables**: Updated ICPs, updated brand/social voice guidelines, content audit, content pillars, 3 content pieces (2 socials, 1 blog)
    </Check>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Week 3: Planning + Calendering">
    - Initiate handover process for relevant channels
    - Shared ownership of content drafting/scheduling for blog/social/ghostwriting
    - Content calendar for next 30 days
    - Evergreen asset library creation if needed (stats, testimonials, stories, top partnerships for amplification)
    - Basic SEO/GEO audit: quick wins and obvious gaps

    <Check>
      **Deliverables**: 1 week of content (~1 medium length blog post, 3 Han LI posts, 3 LI Mintlify posts, 2 Han X posts, 2 Mintlify X posts), high-level content calendar, organized evergreen assets
    </Check>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Week 4: Consistency + Testing">
    - 80-100% ownership of content creation & management for social/blog/ghostwriting
    - Take over community engagement on socials
    - Beginning research and outreach for approved co-marketed content like executive interviews, customer success stories, co-authored industry analysis/best practices guides, etc.)
    - Audit existing email sequence for free trial, start outlining/drafting 5-7 email nurture sequence

    <Check>
      **Deliverables**: 2 weeks of content (~2 medium length blog posts, 6 Han LI posts, 6 LI Mintlify posts, 3 Han X posts, 3 Mintlify X posts), outlines for free trial emails
    </Check>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Week 5-6: Testing + Optimization">
    - Continuing content creation & management
    - Evaluate best-performing content and making updates accordingly
    - Revisions, design/formatting, and review for free trial email nurture sequence

    <Check>
      **Deliverables**: 4 weeks of content (~4 medium length blog posts, 12 Han LI posts, 12-16 Mintlify LI posts, 12 Han X posts, 12-16 Mintlify X posts), email sequence revisions
    </Check>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Week 7-8: Scaling + Expansion">
    - Continuing content creation & management
    - Content calendar for next period
    - Identify areas of opportunity cost/where outsourcing to freelancers may be most efficient  (ie. SEO/GEO support, writing delegation)
    - Work with Tiffany on budget if needed
    - Evaluate other channels/campaigns of interest: ie. Youtube, collaborative campaigns w/ rest of marketing team
    - Final edits, technical setup, testing for email sequence

    <Check>
      **Deliverables**: Deployed email sequence, 4 weeks of content
    </Check>
  </Step>
</Steps>
