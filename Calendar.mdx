<Steps>
  <Step title="Week 1: Onboarding Process" stepNumber={1}>
    - Introductory calls with founders, marketing, product, customer success, technical writer, etc.
    - Tutorials/demos, familiarize further with platform
    - Setting up shared calendar with product team for new features
    - Platform access (CMS, analytics, social, etc.)
    - Review relevant materials, including:
      - Branding guidelines
      - Current marketing practices/past intiatives
      - Competitor analysis
      - Existing customer data
      - Slack community
      - Marketing KPI dashboard 
      - Testimonials and case studies 

    <Check>
      **Deliverables**: Mutual vibes, mostly.
    </Check>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Week 2: Research + Discovery">
    - Set KPIs
    - Exploration calls with power users: 2-3 developers, 2-3 enterprise PMs/technical writers
    - Update user personas
    - Brand voice guidelines update
    - Set target channels
    - Content audit \+ identify content gaps
    - Define new content pillars, content mix
    - Take over performance tracking if needed

    <Check>
      **Deliverables**: Content strategy, ICP profile update
    </Check>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Week 3: Planning + Calendering">
    - Initiate handover process for relevant channels, shared ownership of content creation/scheduling for blog/social/ghostwriting (_note: lower output during handover period_)
    - Content calendar for next 30 days (with systematic increase in posting cadence)
    - Info bank creation (stats, testimonials, stories, top partnerships for amplification)
    - Update social guidelines if needed
    - Basic SEO/GEO audit: quick wins and obvious gaps
    - Review budget with Tiffany \+ any immediate gaps with tools/software
    - Set up basic content approvals process with Tiffany

    <Check>
      **Deliverables**: High-level content calendar, organized evergreen assets, update social media guides
    </Check>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Week 4: Ownership + Testing">
    - 70-100% ownership of content creation & management for social/blog/ghostwriting
    - Take over community engagement on socials
    - Beginning research and outreach for co-marketed content like executive interviews, customer success stories, co-authored industry analysis, best practices guides, etc.)

    <Check>
      **Deliverables**: 2 weeks of content (~2 medium length blog posts, 6 Han LI posts, 6 LI Mintlify posts, 
    </Check>
    <Note>
      Though using freelancers to fill the content gap is possible, I'd prefer to test self-producing content for the initial ramp-up to get a feel of the workflow and style before bringing on any additional writers.
    </Note>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Week 5-6: Testing + Optimization">
    - Continuing content creation & management, gradually scaling up posting frequency
    - Evaluate best-performing content and making updates accordingly

    <Check>
      4 weeks of content (~4 medium length blog posts, 12 Han LI posts, 6 Mintlify LI posts
    </Check>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Week 7-8: Scaling + Expansion">
    - Content calendar for next period
    - Identify areas of opportunity cost/where outsourcing to freelancers may be most efficient  (ie. SEO/GEO support, writing delegation)
    - Evaluate other channels/campaigns of interest: ie. Youtube, newsletters, collaborative campaigns w/ rest of marketing team
  </Step>
</Steps>
